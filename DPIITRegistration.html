DPIIT Registration

Government certification for startups under the Startup India initiative

What is DPIIT Registration?

DPIIT (Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade) recognition is a government certification for startups in India. It allows your business to enjoy special benefits under the Startup India initiative.

Eligibility Criteria

Who Can Apply?

1

The Startup should be incorporated as a private limited company or registered as a partnership firm or a limited liability partnership.

2

Turnover should be less than INR 100 Crores in any of the previous financial years.

3

An entity shall be considered as a startup up to 10 years from the date of its incorporation.

4

The Startup should be working towards innovation/improvement of existing products, services and processes and should have the potential to generate employment/create wealth. An entity formed by splitting up or reconstruction of an existing business shall not be considered a "Startup".

Key Advantages

Benefits of DPIIT Registration

Tax Exemption

100% profit tax exemption for 3 consecutive years under Section 80-IAC

Easier Funding

Access to government-backed funds and schemes

IPR Benefits

Fast-track patents, trademarks, and reduced fees

Regulatory Relaxation

Exemptions from certain labor and compliance requirements

Government Tenders

Special privileges in government procurement

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Startup India Registration and DPIIT Recognition?
Startup India Registration is the initial step where you register on the Startup India portal. DPIIT Recognition is the official government certification that provides access to tax benefits, funding opportunities, and other schemes under the Startup India initiative.
How long does it take to get DPIIT recognition?
DPIIT recognition is typically granted within 2-5 working days if all documents are in order and the application meets the eligibility criteria. The process is entirely online through the Startup India portal.
What documents are required for DPIIT registration?
Required documents include Certificate of Incorporation/Registration, PAN card of the entity, brief description of innovation/business model, pitch deck or business plan, and details of directors/partners. You may also need to provide proof of funding or patent/trademark details if applicable.
Can a sole proprietorship apply for DPIIT recognition?
No, sole proprietorships are not eligible for DPIIT recognition. The entity must be incorporated as a Private Limited Company, registered as a Partnership Firm, or formed as a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP).
How do I claim tax exemption under Section 80-IAC?
After obtaining DPIIT recognition, you need to apply for tax exemption separately through the Inter-Ministerial Board (IMB). Once approved, you can claim 100% tax exemption on profits for any 3 consecutive years out of the first 10 years from incorporation.
Is DPIIT recognition valid for lifetime?
DPIIT recognition is valid for up to 10 years from the date of incorporation of the entity. After 10 years, the entity will no longer be considered a startup and will lose the associated benefits.
Can a DPIIT recognized startup raise funds easily?
Yes, DPIIT recognized startups get access to various government schemes like Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS), Credit Guarantee Scheme, and are eligible to apply for seed funding through the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS). This recognition also increases credibility with investors.
What is the IPR benefit for DPIIT startups?
DPIIT recognized startups get 80% rebate on patent filing fees and 50% rebate on trademark filing fees. They also benefit from fast-track examination of patent applications and access to a panel of facilitators for IPR filing assistance, with government bearing facilitator fees.
