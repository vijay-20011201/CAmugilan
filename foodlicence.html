What is FSSAI Registration?

If you are planning to start a food business in Bangalore or anywhere in India, obtaining a Food License (FSSAI) is mandatory.

With MKDA's experienced consultants, get a hassle-free FSSAI license process.

FSSAI was established under the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006, governed by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.

  • Every food business requires an FSSAI license/registration
  • Mandatory for manufacturers, distributors, restaurants, street vendors
  • Applies to small, medium, and large operators
  • 14-digit registration/license number must be displayed on packaging
  • Overseen by Chairperson & CEO of FSSAI

Types of FSSAI Registrations

Basic Registration

For small businesses/startups (turnover < ₹12 lakh). Upgradable to State as business grows.

State License

For mid-sized companies (turnover ₹12–20 crore). Issued by State Government; valid in one state.

Central License

For large businesses/importers (turnover > ₹20 crore). Issued by Central Government.

FSSAI Registration Procedure

Based on business volume, capacity, turnover, and location.

  1. Submit application to Food Safety Department
  2. Department reviews & accepts/rejects within 7 days (rejection in writing)
  3. If accepted, certificate issued with registration number & photo
  4. FBO must display certificate prominently during business hours

Documents Required (Central License)

  • Form B (completed & signed)
  • List of Directors/Partners/Proprietor with address, contact, photo ID
  • List of food categories
  • Partnership deed/proprietorship (Shop & Establishment, GST, etc.)
  • Form IX
  • Proof of turnover & transportation
  • Declaration form
  • Rental Agreement & latest electricity bill of premises
  • Passport size photos & contact details of owners/directors

Frequently Asked Questions

Does homemade food require FSSAI Licence?
Yes, you need a food license from FSSAI to sell homemade food, but only registration is required if it is a small scale
What is the full form of FSSAI?
The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI).
Who needs a FSSAI license?
FSSAI Registration is a basic license that is mandatory for all the FBOs involved in the small-scale food business. FSSAI covers the following businesses: Any FBO with an annual turnover of not more than Rs. 12 lakh.

Is FSSAI mandatory?
Obtaining an FSSAI license is compulsory before starting any food business operation in India. The traders, manufacturers, restaurants involved in the food business must obtain a 14-digit license number that is printed on their food products.
