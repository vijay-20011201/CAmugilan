Frequently Asked Questions

What do payroll services do?
Payroll services pay salaries to a company's employees. It starts with preparing a list of employees to be paid and ends with recording those expenses. It's a tangled process that needs different payroll, HR, and finance teams to work together.
Do I need payroll services?
Based on the business entity you own, you might be required to give yourself a salary as an employee. You will have to run payroll to pay yourself and any other employees.
What are the benefits of being on payroll services?
Refer the above section under Advantages that companies get by outsourcing payroll services
What is the difference between salary and payroll?
Salary is the amount of pay an employee earns, whereas payroll refers to the system employers use to process salary payments
What is an outsourcing company?
Outsourcing is the business practice of hiring a party outside your company to perform in-house services. It is a practice usually undertaken by companies as a cost-cutting measure.
